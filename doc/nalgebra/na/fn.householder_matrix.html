Function nalgebra::na::householder_matrix[src]

pub fn householder_matrix<N: Float, M: Eye + Indexable<(uint, uint), N>, V: Indexable<uint, N>>(dim: uint, start: uint, vec: V) -> M

Get the householder matrix corresponding to a reflexion to the hyperplane defined by `vec̀ . It can be a reflexion contained in a subspace.

 Arguments

  • dim - the dimension of the space the resulting matrix operates in
  • start - the starting dimension of the subspace of the reflexion
  • vec - the vector defining the reflection.
